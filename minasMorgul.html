Minas Morgul, formerly the beautiful Minas Ithil, now serves as a haunting fortress radiating a ghostly light. Under the control of the Ringwraiths, it marks the path to the Morgul Vale and the passage to Mount Doom. Here, Frodo and Sam witness the terrifying spectacle of the Witch-king leading his army, emphasizing the fortress's role as a fearsome gateway and a critical point in their perilous journey to destroy the Ring.
