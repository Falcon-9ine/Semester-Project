Morannon, known as the Black Gate of Mordor, stands as a daunting entrance to Sauron's realm, flanked by massive battlements and guarded by orcs. Symbolizing Sauron's power, it's also the site of the critical Battle of the Morannon, where the armies of the West gather in a bold stand to distract Sauron, aiding Frodo and Sam's covert mission to destroy the Ring. This fierce confrontation highlights the Fellowship's bravery and strategic sacrifice, underscoring the stark contrasts between the forces of light and darkness.
