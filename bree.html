Bree

  • Back to Map

Bree is a bustling village notable for being one of the few places in Middle-earth where Hobbits and Men live side by side. Situated at the crossroads of major routes, Bree thrives on trade and the exchange of news and stories among travelers. The village is best known for The Prancing Pony, an inn that provides a warm welcome to weary travelers with its hearty meals and cozy lodgings. The atmosphere in Bree is one of cautious curiosity, as the diverse inhabitants and visitors bring tales from distant lands, making it a pivotal location for adventurers beginning their journeys into the wilder parts of the world.
